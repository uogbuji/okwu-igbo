Sélénsé



"Sélénsé" is a classic Igbo woman's folk song (ụ́mụ̀ àgbọ́ghọ́ n'emé sélénsé):

Onye ihe m n'ewe iwe ya biko wegbuo ya
Sélénsé
Osukosu nwa mkpi ya biko sugbuo ya
Sélénsé

Chinelo Okparanta in "Under the Udala Trees" narrates how a young girl doing this, when she says "selense", "moved her hips sharply from side to side, jutting her face in a pose, as if getting ready for her picture to be taken."
